# Onboarding — choose your account type

Right after signing up, users land here to choose what kind of account they need. There are three options:

- **Tenant** — for people looking for a place to rent. Choosing this takes them to complete their profile so they can sign contracts later.
- **Freelance** — for independent property managers who handle properties on their own.
- **Real estate agency** — for agencies that manage properties as a team.

The freelance and agency options set up the user's subscription type and organization automatically. Tenants just go straight to filling in their profile.
