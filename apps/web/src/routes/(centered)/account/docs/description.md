# User profile

This is where users manage their personal information and documents. They can update their name, surname, phone number, document number, and CUIL, then click "Guardar información" to save.

There's also a documents section where users can click "Agregar documento" to upload personal files like DNI, passport, or credit reports, and "Descargar" to download them. These documents are used in various processes across the platform, like contract creation.
