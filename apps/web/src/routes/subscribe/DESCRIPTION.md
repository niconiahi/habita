# Subscription management

This is where users manage their platform subscription. The page adapts to the user's situation:

- **Active subscription** — shows a green card confirming the subscription is active, along with the expiration date.
- **Expired or grace period** — shows a warning and a payment button. Freelance accounts pay a flat $50 USD, while real estate agencies pay $40 USD per team member seat.
- **Non-admin members** — if a team member's subscription expired but they're not the account admin, they'll see a message telling them their administrator needs to renew it.

The payment button redirects to Mercado Pago to complete the transaction.
