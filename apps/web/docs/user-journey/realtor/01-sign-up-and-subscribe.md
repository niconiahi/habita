# Realtor: Sign up and subscribe for the agency

## The Lens

**Actor:** Real estate agency owner — someone who runs a property management agency with a team.

**Goal:** Create an agency account on the platform and pay for the team subscription.

**Scenario:** An agency owner discovers Habita and wants to manage their properties and team through the platform.

---

## The Experience

### Phase 1: Create Account

**Routes:** `/signup`

**Actions:**
- Fills in name, surname, email, password (or Google signup)
- Gets redirected to onboarding

**Thoughts:**
- "Can my whole team use this?"
- "How is this different from freelance?"

**Emotions:** Evaluating → Proceeding

---

### Phase 2: Choose Agency Account

**Routes:** `/onboarding`

**Actions:**
- Sees three options
- Clicks "Real estate agency" — described as "manage properties as an agency with your team"
- System creates the organization and subscription

**Thoughts:**
- "This is for teams — that's me"
- "What does the organization include?"

**Emotions:** Clear → Committed

---

### Phase 3: Pay for Team

**Routes:** `/subscribe` → Mercado Pago → `/subscribe/success`

**Actions:**
- Sees pricing: $40 USD per team member seat
- Reviews the total based on seat count
- Completes payment via Mercado Pago

**Thoughts:**
- "How much will this cost as my team grows?"
- "Is each person a separate seat?"

**Emotions:** Calculating → Invested (access unlocked)

---

## The Output

### Pain Points
- Per-seat pricing is unclear before choosing the account type
- No volume discounts for larger teams
- Initial seat count may not match the desired team size

### Opportunities
- Show pricing calculator before committing
- Offer volume discounts
