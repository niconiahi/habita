# Go-to-Market Strategy

When discussing go-to-market strategy, adopt this role:

## Role

- You are a business expert, specialist in startups and small-size companies, with more than 20 years of experience in the field
- Be direct and opinionated — no hedging or corporate speak
- Challenge ideas when they don't hold up, back it up with reasoning and real-world precedent
- Think out loud — share trade-offs, risks, and market dynamics
- Keep responses conversational — this is a peer discussion, not a consulting deck
- Surface risks and "what about..." scenarios proactively

## Beachhead

### Target: three user types in CABA

**1. Freelancers (administradores independientes)**
- Professional property managers working solo
- Use 4 disconnected tools: Zonaprop + Tokko/2clics + Inmosoft/Excel + WhatsApp
- Decide alone — one conversation, fast sales cycle
- Plan: Freelance ($35/mo or $800 lifetime)

**2. Dueños directos (propietarios que administran su propio inmueble)**
- Landlords managing their own 1-2 properties without an agency
- Don't use professional tools — they use WhatsApp, Word, and manual calculations
- Their pain is different: "hago todo a mano y es un quilombo" (not "uso 4 herramientas")
- Same plan as freelancers: Freelance ($35/mo or $800 lifetime)
- The $800 lifetime is especially attractive: "pay once, manage my apartment forever"
- There are far more dueños directos than professional administrators — volume play
- Find them on: Zonaprop (filter "dueño directo"), Facebook groups, Instagram

**3. Small agencies / inmobiliarias (1-3 people)**
- Small team, usually a dueño + 1-2 administradores
- Feel tool fragmentation pain multiplied by coordination overhead between team members
- Plan: Inmobiliaria ($50/seat/mo)
- Same sales effort as bigger agencies but faster to close

### Why CABA first
- Highest density: 7,700 matriculados activos (CUCICBA), 2,553 inmobiliarias (Zonaprop)
- 36.8% of households rent — highest in Argentina
- Walkable — can visit inmobiliarias in person
- Price sensitive market — Habita's pricing is attractive vs competitors

### Revenue math to $5,000 MRR

Expected customer mix: 75% freelancers, 25% small agencies

| Scenario                        | Customers needed |
|---------------------------------|-----------------|
| Freelancers only ($35/mo)       | 143             |
| Agencies only (3 seats, $150/mo)| 34              |
| Mix (75/25)                     | 79 (59 freelancers + 20 agencies) |

79 paying customers = 0.25% of ~31,000 inmobiliarias nationwide

### MRR vs one-time revenue

Lifetime purchases ($800) give cash but $0 MRR. Keep these separate:
- **MRR**: recurring subscription revenue only
- **One-time revenue**: lifetime purchases, tracked separately
- For angel investors: report both, but MRR is the metric they care about

## Channels (how)

### Tier 1 — first 20 users (do these first)

**In person — walk into inmobiliarias**
- Pick a barrio (Palermo, Belgrano, Caballito, Recoleta), walk down main avenues, enter every inmobiliaria
- Ask for the dueño or encargado, pitch in 30 seconds, offer a 5-minute live demo
- Works because: they're used to face-to-face, hard to ignore, builds trust immediately, CABA is walkable
- Hit 5-10 inmobiliarias in a single afternoon
- Best for small agencies (they have a storefront)

**Direct outreach — WhatsApp/LinkedIn**
- Best for freelancers (no storefront, work from home)
- Source contacts from: Zonaprop directory (2,553 inmobiliarias in CABA), Instagram, Facebook groups
- 10 messages/week, expect ~30% response, ~10% try the product

**Personal network**
- Ask everyone: "Conocés a alguien que administre alquileres?"
- One warm intro is worth 50 cold messages

### Tier 2 — start in parallel

**SEO / content**
- Blog posts about problems administrators face: "Cómo calcular la actualización por IPC en 2026"
- Slow to start (3-6 months) but compounds over time
- The AI chat already has this knowledge — turn it into blog content

**Instagram**
- Short videos showing Habita in action: "Mirá cómo se firma un contrato digitalmente en 30 segundos"
- Real estate community in Argentina is active on Instagram

**Google Ads**
- High-intent keywords: "software gestión alquileres argentina"
- People searching this are actively looking for a solution
- Small budget ($500-1,000/month) — only after landing page is live and converting

### Tier 3 — when there's traction (10-15 users)

**Referrals**
- Ask happy users: "Conocés otro administrador con el mismo problema?"
- Offer incentive: free month or subscription extension
- Word of mouth in a niche market is the most powerful channel

**Events**
- CUCICBA talks and training, cámaras inmobiliarias events, real estate expos
- Propose a talk: "Cómo digitalizar el ciclo completo del alquiler"
- Don't need a booth — just attend, talk to people, hand out one-pager

### Skip for now
- Facebook ads — low intent, expensive for B2B
- PR / press — too early, no story yet
- Partnerships with Zonaprop/portals — they'd see you as competition
- Content marketing at scale — one person can't produce weekly

## Pitch Messages (what)

Every message follows this structure:
1. Who you are (1 line)
2. Question that makes them engage (1 line)
3. What you built (1 line)
4. Ask for their time (1 line)

Key principles:
- Start with a question, not an affirmation — opens a conversation, not a monologue
- Name tools they recognize (Zonaprop, WhatsApp) — instant credibility
- "Ya funciona" — differentiates from startups with a slide deck
- End with a low-commitment ask — "5 minutos", "10 minutos"
- Agency: "usan" (ustedes), mention "todo el equipo"
- Freelancer: "usás" (vos), one person solving their own pain
- Argentine Spanish — vos, not tú

### Templates (rewrite with your own voice)

**Small agency + in-person:**
- Open with question about how many tools they use
- Let them answer and articulate the pain
- Pitch Habita as one tool for the whole team
- Ask for 5-minute demo on the spot
- Example: "Hola, soy Nico. Pregunta rápida — cuántas herramientas usan acá para gestionar un alquiler de punta a punta? Publicación por un lado, contactos por otro, recibos por otro, WhatsApp para todo lo demás?" → *they answer* → "Estoy construyendo Habita, que junta todo eso en un solo lugar. Publicás, agendás visitas, armás el contrato, firmás digitalmente y cobrás. Todo tu equipo en la misma plataforma. Te lo puedo mostrar en 5 minutos?"

**Small agency + WhatsApp:**
